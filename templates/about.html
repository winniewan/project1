Our message

Sometimes complications may arise where we cannot find you an event or activity taking place near you that falls under your preferred activities. To solve this we offer something outside of your comfort zone in the hopes for you to try something new!

This is a personal project from a group of 5 undergraduate students from the University of Rochester. We aim to create something similar to reddit, except it's cnitt! As U of R students, there are several opinions and ideas and we would love to create a platform that allows anyone to share, communicate, and learn from each other.
